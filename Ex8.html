Conclusion:

1. Value of correlation lies between -1 to 1(-1 < cor < 1). Closer the value of correlation to 1 or -1, more the model depends on the selected columns and closer the value of correlation to 0, the lesser the model depends on the selected column.

2. At the end of the file, in the summary of model2, higher the number of stars, more the column can affect the models prediction(more the column charges depends on the particular column).
